Project 2: Business Details App

The UK Business Details App is an interactive map-based tool that visualizes the names and addresses of UK importers and exporters trading with **non-EU countries** and **GB importers/exporters trading with the EU** in 2022 and 2023.

This app supports **tariff and Rules of Origin analysis** by identifying which businesses trade specific products, aiding **supply chain analysis**, and highlighting their **geographical distribution** across the UK for **regional policy insights**.

Key Data Science Elements:

  • Geospatial Analysis & Mapping: Utilized leaflet and sf to visualize business locations across the UK.
  • Data Integration & Processing: Merged **business registration data, trade statistics, and tariff information** to create a comprehensive dataset.
  • Interactive Visualization: Developed **clickable map markers** where users can explore **business names, traded sectors, product codes, and trade dates**.
  • Time-Series Filtering: Users can **toggle between 2022 and 2023 data** to analyze trends over time.
  • Supply Chain & Regional Insights: Identifies **key UK businesses by industry and location**, supporting trade and policy research.

Impact & Skills Demonstrated:

  • Data-Driven Policy Insights: Helps policymakers identify **trade clusters, supply chain dependencies, and potential impacts of new trade policies**.
  • Geospatial Data Science: Demonstrates proficiency in **mapping, spatial data processing, and interactive geographic analytics**.
  • End-to-End Data Engineering: Includes **data cleaning, merging, visualization, and performance optimization**.
