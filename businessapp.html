Project 2: Business Details App

The UK Business Details App is an interactive mapping tool that visualizes the names and addresses of UK importers and exporters trading with **non-EU countries** and **GB importers/exporters trading with the EU** in 2022 and 2023.

This app supports **tariff and Rules of Origin analysis** by identifying which businesses trade specific products, aiding **supply chain analysis**, and highlighting their **geographical distribution** across the UK for **regional policy insights**.

Key Data Science Elements:

  • Geospatial Analysis & Mapping: Used leaflet and sf to visualize business locations.
  • Data Processing & Integration: Combined **business registration data, trade statistics, and tariff information** into a structured dataset.
  • Interactive Visualization: Developed **clickable map markers** to display **business names, sectors, traded products, and trade dates**.
  • Time-Series Filtering: Users can switch between **2022 and 2023 data** to analyze trade trends.
  • Supply Chain & Regional Insights: Identifies key UK businesses by industry and location, supporting trade policy research.

Impact & Skills Demonstrated:

  • Data-Driven Insights: Helps policymakers analyze **trade patterns, supply chain dependencies, and regional trade distribution**.
  • Geospatial Data Science: Demonstrates **mapping, spatial data processing, and interactive geographic analytics**.
  • End-to-End Data Engineering: Showcases skills in **data cleaning, merging, and interactive visualization**.
